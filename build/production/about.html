Our Team

  • Our History

Ryan Clapper and Bennett Roth met in college due to their mutual love of finance along with their shared interest in football. They realized that there was a lack of financial firms that stood out to clients and offered truly unique services. So along with their mutual friend, Chris Levine, who majored in Computer Science they came together and founded Accuracy in the fall of 2018. Together we combine our love for technology and finances to bring clients a simple yet easy digital economic system to help better manage their businesses. We have noticed that since we opened our doors that many financial agencies tend to prioritize making money over customers needs. That's why we're proud to say that we 100% prioritize customer needs over financial gain.

We begin our Journey

March 1, 2018

Bennett Roth, Chris Levine and Ryan Clapper, founded Accuracy in Atlanta, Georgia. They were founded upon the principles of communication, inspiration and global commerce.

Our First Client

March 15, 2018

After a couple of weeks of advertising, Accuracy made their first corporate agreement to do business planning and financial accounting for a company based out of Alpharetta, Georgia.

First 100 Clients

June 10, 2018

We reached the milestone of 100 clients after 3 months of being open. A majority of our clients use our All-Star package which is our best deal since it combines all three of our other packages.

$500,000 in Net Profit

October 30, 2018

Throughout the month of October, we saw our biggest increase in Client base. This lead to us being able to bring in our largest amount of monthly revenue leading us to reach our first revenue milestone.

New York Office

January 10, 2019

Once we realized that we need to expand our global presence, we decided to open an office close to Wall Street. Our office was strategically placed so we can understand the progression of the Stock Market and be close to some of our customers.

Projected 2 Million in Net Profit

May 1, 2019

We plan on approaching the two million revenue dollar milestone. We have made a steady increase in Clients and employees over the past 3 months, so that is why we decided on this date.
