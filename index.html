Frequently Asked Questions

What Payments Methods do you Accept?

We accept PayPal and all major credit cards & debit cards online. We also accept eChecks online.

Are there any minimum subscription lengths?

As of right now, we allow any company to use our services for as long as they need until they are able to succeed again.

How quickly will I have access to my portfolio?

At the beginning of your subscription to Accuracy we will send a secure email with a username and a temporary password until you change it to your own.

Am I allowed to combine multiple packages?

You are allowed to combine any of the packages you want, but we recommend the All-Star since it combines all others thus making it the best value.

How much input will I have on my portfolio and accounting?

As much or as little as you want, we believe that our customers should have as much flexibility as they want. This separates us from the competition because we allow our customers to choose their own paths in accounting.

When are payments due?

We will send you a total bill for all of your services during a month on the last day of that month and you pay then.
