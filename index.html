Frequently Asked Questions

What Payment Methods do you Accept?

We accept PayPal and all major credit cards & debit cards online. We also accept eChecks online.

Are there any minimum subscription lengths?

As of now, we allow any company to use our services for as long as they need.

How quickly will I have access to my portfolio?

At the beginning of your subscription to Accuracy, we will send a secure email with a user name and a temporary password to access your account.

Am I allowed to combine multiple packages?

You are able to combine any of the packages we currently offer, but we recommend the All-Star as it combines all of our other packages into one.

How much input will I have?

Here at Accuracy, we believe that users should have as little or much input as you want with your financials, that's why we have a dedicated work staff that works around the clock for you.

When are payments due?

Payments are due at the end of the month. At that time, an invoice for your service will be sent to you along with a link to our secure payment portal to complete your transaction.
